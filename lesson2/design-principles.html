PARC: Repetition

SNOWBOARDER

Website link

The PARC: Repetition on the Snowboarder website is the use of the same words or phrases multiple times within a short space. This can be seen in the use of the word "snowboard" in the title of the website, as well as in the subheadings and body copy. For example, the subheading "The Latest Snowboard News" contains the word "snowboard" twice, and the body copy contains the phrase "snowboarding videos and photos" three times.

White Space and Clean Design

FERIA SAN MARCOS

Website link

The Feria San Marcos website uses white space and clean design to create a visually appealing and easy-to-use experience for visitors. The use of white space helps to make the website feel spacious and uncluttered, while the clean design helps to make the content easy to read and understand.
The website uses a light color palette with plenty of white space, which helps to create a sense of openness and spaciousness.

PARC: Contrast

TRAVIS ONLINE

Website link

The Travis Online site offer an awesome contrast with a very dark red in the background and a very soft yellow to hightlight lettres and buttons. The colour combination has an amazing contrast with the fotos shoen in the site. The secondary letters show an white color to give a contrast with the white spaces.
