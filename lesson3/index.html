Site Purpose

The Aguascalientes City Chamber of Commerce is a non-profit organization that represents the interests of businesses in the city and Aguascalientes State in Mexico. We work to promote economic development, improve the business environment, and advocate for the interests of our members.
Our mission is to create a thriving business community that benefits all of Aguascalientes commerce community. We do this by:


  • Providing resources and support to businesses
  • Advocating for business interests at the local, state, and federal levels
  • Promoting economic development and job creation
  • Strengthening the business community through education and networking

We are committed to working with businesses of all sizes to help them succeed. If you are a business owner in Aguascalientes, we encourage you to join our chamber. We are here to help you grow your business and reach your full potential.
